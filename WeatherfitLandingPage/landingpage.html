Why WeatherFit?

Choosing what to wear based only on a weather forecast can often lead to discomfort — it's hard to judge if an outfit will feel right throughout the day. WeatherFit solves this by analyzing the full-day temperature range and recommending clothing options that keep you comfortable and stylish.

App Features

Real-Time Weather Detection

WeatherFit locates your position and gathers precise temperature, wind, and weather information to personalize your outfit recommendations.

Smart Outfit Suggestions

Based on a clothing library tagged with ideal temperature ranges, WeatherFit recommends diverse and appropriate outfit combinations.

Interactive Shake-to-Refresh

Want a new look? Simply shake your phone to refresh the outfit recommendation and discover more styles!

Add your favorite

Users can collect their favorite daily combinations

Outfit Collection & Community

Save your favorite outfits matched to different weather conditions and explore outfit ideas shared by others in the WeatherFit community.
